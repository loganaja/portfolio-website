THE OBJECTIVE

Upon receiving management approval, I was given a strict budget and timeline to scale the refined design into a final production run of 11 operational units: 5 standalone portable testers and a 6-unit integrated test bench network.

THE EXECUTION

Serving as the project lead, I managed the entire lifecycle from generating the comprehensive Bill of Materials (BOM) to completing the full mechanical and electrical cabinet CAD designs. I selected and integrated a 10-inch touchscreen interface to eliminate external peripherals, iterated on the enclosure for noise reduction, and collaborated closely with senior engineers and electricians to bridge technical gaps in industrial compliance.

THE IMPACT

I successfully delivered all 11 production units (5 individual, 6 bench-mounted), resulting in a compact, quiet, and highly maintainable fleet that standardized syringe durability testing across the facility.
