THE OBJECTIVE

In addition to executing core project milestones, I was tasked with managing rapid prototyping infrastructure, supporting multi-disciplinary engineering teams, and driving operational efficiency across Trajan’s research and manufacturing departments.

THE EXECUTION

I was apart of the deployment, calibration, and optimization of the department's upgraded Bambu 3D printer fleet to enhance in-house rapid prototyping capabilities. To foster a highly collaborative R&D environment, I partnered with fellow interns to peer-review cross-examine mechanical designs, providing unique engineering perspectives that solved complex design issues. I conducted rigorous data collection and statistical analysis on production machinery to validate product accuracy, shadowed senior engineers on high-level industrial projects, and fabricated custom mechanical components Furthermore, I collaborated directly with the in-house machine shop to learn practical machining constraints, and managed external vendor relations coordinating with suppliers and sales teams to analyze component pricing, audit procurement lead times, and eliminate supply-chain bottlenecks before they could delay active projects.

THE IMPACT

My proactive support minimized project downtime and accelerated the R&D prototyping.
