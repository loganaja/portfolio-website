THE OBJECTIVE

During the final production stages of the Lapping 4 machine framework, I was tasked with implementing design enhancements to improve usability, prevent fluid leaks, and standardize components with the newer Lapping 5 model.

THE EXECUTION

I designed newly modified upper and lower machine doors to ensure geometric cross-compatibility across both machine generations. To resolve operational fluid containment issues, I engineered improved, leak-proof drip trays. I managed procurement by generating a comprehensive Bill of Materials (BOM) for the pneumatic systems and structural aluminum lighting fixtures, integrated aesthetic protective covers, and worked alongside production technicians to install modules and prep the machine indexer for final assembly.

THE IMPACT

My enhancements significantly improved the machine's aesthetics and operational containment, while part standardization streamlined the assembly pipeline and ensured consistency across the manufacturing floor.
