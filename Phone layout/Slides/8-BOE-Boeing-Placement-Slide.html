THE OBJECTIVE

Appointed to the Advanced Manufacturing R&D and Collaborative Robotics (Cobots) team at Boeing Aerostructures, I was tasked with supporting high-level aerospace manufacturing initiatives and driving my own dedicated research project focused on validating next-generation manufacturing technologies.

THE EXECUTION

I collaborated closely with multidisciplinary senior engineers to design and execute rigorous testing procedures, set up physical experiments, and develop high-precision CAD models in Catia V5 for aerospace tooling and robotic integration. I was assigned an independent project investigating future manufacturing technologies. I took full ownership of this initiative, engineering the testing parameters, executing the experimental phases, and performing comprehensive data collection to analyze the technology's viability. Concluding my placement, I took these complex findings into a technical presentation and delivered it directly to the entire Boeing Aerostructures engineering division.

THE IMPACT

My research successfully delivered validated, high-integrity data that informed Boeing’s future advanced manufacturing roadmap, while demonstrating my ability to independently work on projects and communicate technical insights to large-scale aerospace engineering teams.
